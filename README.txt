CERN ATLAS NSW Micromegas detectors high voltage power supply data logger,
for project "ATLAS New Small Wheel Micromegas production at Joint Institute for Nuclear Research".

Software: LabVIEW 2018 32bit

Hardware:|--> 1) CAEN SY5527 is used for testing detectors with HV quality.
         |--> 2) Arduino and BMP085 are used for environment monitoring.
